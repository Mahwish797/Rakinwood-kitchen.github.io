About Us

We are providing one window solution for home décor including Modern and Classical German &Italian Kitchens, Italian Furniture, Tiles& Sanitary. We focus on innovative design, reliability, durability, commitment and success. We are working in interior design industry for more than a decade and have covered many projects with the collaboration of key architects and the builders. We are pioneers in the home décor industry offering the market greater choice, state of the art technology and ergonomic designs as well as environment friendly furniture. Rakinwood has focused on its core principles of customer service, quality and strategic after sales service.

HISTORY

It’s a story of decade, it’s an account of an uphill task converted into mere peanuts through a clear vision, sheer dedication, meticulous hard work and consistent belief.

Rainkins was incorporated in the year 2006 and started with the Italian kitchens. In the same years we had diversified in two new areas including Italian furniture and wide range of Tiles & Sanitary to fulfil the needs of array of clientele for home décor.

Rainkins is an iconic provider of the Italian Kitchens serving in Pakistan. The business was originally incorporated over 10 years ago and has undergone various changes in business strategy. The refined model is to import exclusive range of high quality products and sell primarily to the customers.

We also specialize in High Quality Furniture, Spanish Tiles in cooperating all the latest designs and styles from Italy and Span

Kitchens are undeniably the heart of today’s homes so, understandably, you want your kitchen to look its best. Even if you aren’t in the market for a total re-do, one or two trendy updates will bring your kitchen into the 21st century.
